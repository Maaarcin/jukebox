GRUNGE.
Grunge is a common definition of alternative rock music that was created around Seattle (USA) in the 80's. The popularity of this genre peaked at the beginning of the 90s with the release of NIRVANA's "Nevermind". So called The Big Four of Grunge includes the NIRVANA, PEARL JAM, SOUNDGARDEN, ALICE IN CHAINS. The phenomenon of this music consisted in combining the characteristic dirty sound of guitars, punk rhythms and melodies. Other bands: Green River, Mad Season, Melvins, Mudhoney, Screeming Trees, Temple Of The Dog, Mother Love Bone, Stone Temple Pilots, Hole and many more.
